About Me

Get to know the person behind the professional.



My Journey

I am a dedicated professional with a foundational background in **IT Support and Technical Services**. I spent over 3 years immersed in troubleshooting and backend operations, where my focus was on system reliability and integrity. This experience taught me discipline, meticulous problem-solving, and client communication—skills that are now invaluable in my career transition to **Full-Stack Web Development**.

My foundational skills in Python, Java, and Networking, combined with intensive training in the **MERN stack** (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js) from StackTrek, have equipped me to handle the entire development lifecycle, from database design to front-end implementation.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • **Backend Reliability**: Deep experience in IT troubleshooting, Freshdesk ticket management, and ensuring transaction integrity in production environments.
  • **Development**: Proficient in JavaScript, PHP, and modern web development practices (HTML5, CSS3).
  • **System Administration**: Familiar with Active Directory, Windows OS environments, and remote support tools.
